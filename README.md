# Trip Expense Monitor

A smart trip planner and expense tracker to manage group travel budgets. It helps you monitor spending, split costs, and generates AI-powered travel itineraries to avoid overspending and simplify your trips.

## Features

### Core Trip Management
- **Create Trips:** Easily set up a new trip with details like name, destination, dates, and per-person budget.
- **Trip Dashboard:** View all your upcoming and past trips in an organized, card-based layout.
- **Detailed Trip View:** Dive into a specific trip to see a comprehensive overview of its finances and details.

### Expense Tracking & Management
- **Log Expenses:** Quickly add expenses with a description, amount, category (Food, Transport, etc.), and specify who paid.
- **Real-time Expense List:** See an up-to-date list of all transactions for a trip.
- **Categorical Breakdown:** A dynamic pie chart visualizes where the money is going, helping you understand spending habits.

### Collaborative Budgeting
- **Add Trip Members:** Easily add friends and family to your trip.
- **Automated Balance Splitting:** The app automatically calculates who owes whom, taking the headache out of settling up.
- **Budget at a Glance:** Key stats like Total Budget, Total Spent, and Remaining Funds are prominently displayed.

### AI-Powered Assistance (Powered by Gemini)
- **AI Itinerary Planner:** Generate a personalized, day-by-day travel plan based on your destination, trip duration, and budget. It suggests activities, meals, and estimated costs.
- **AI Budget Predictor:** Get an intelligent budget estimate for your trip. The AI analyzes your destination and trip length to suggest a realistic per-person budget with a justification.

### User Experience & Technical
- **Responsive Design:** Fully functional and looks great on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
- **Light & Dark Mode:** Switch between themes for comfortable viewing in any lighting condition.
- **Persistent Data:** Your trip data is saved locally in your browser, so you can pick up right where you left off.
- **Intuitive Interface:** Clean modals and a clear layout make managing your trips simple and efficient.
